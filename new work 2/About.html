About The city boys

Let’s put it this way. We have about 78 awards that we could put on display but we’re the kind of people who would rather use that space to store snacks. Or hide bodies. Of clients. We’re digressing. All jokes aside, Good People is really just a group of good people who are in love with what they do. Our producers would donate blood (and if needed, sweat) to get the camera rolling on time while our directors are as eclectic as they get. But hey, don’t take our word for it. Ask the crazy people that made us Agency of the Year for three years straight. We’re a production house that says ‘Amen’ to solid work and invites great briefs and money-owning-film-loving people to our dinner table. Creatively, we keep it fresh and solid. Always. Until you come get your fingers orange on some Cheetos when you visit us, have a look at our work. It’ll do the talking.
